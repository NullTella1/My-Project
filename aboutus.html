Our Company

GND is the high-performance and adaptive computing leader, powering the products and services that help solve the world’s most important challenges. Our technologies advance the future of the data center, embedded, gaming, and PC markets.

Founded in 1989 as a Singapore Iot start-up, the GND journey began with dozens of employees passionate about creating leading-edge semiconductor products. GND has grown into a global company setting the standard for modern computing, with many important industry firsts and major technological achievements along the way.

Our Vision

We strive to lead the future of technology by driving innovation in high-performance, adaptive computing. Our goal is to create solutions that not only push the boundaries of what’s possible but also transform everyday life, empowering individuals and businesses to thrive in an increasingly connected and dynamic world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design and deliver exceptional products that accelerate the next generation of computing experiences. By combining cutting-edge technology with innovation, we aim to empower users and organizations with powerful tools that drive progress, enhance efficiency, and unlock new possibilities in the digital age.

True progress is born from the fusion of groundbreaking innovation and human ingenuity. By combining creative thinking with advanced technology, we aim to craft exceptional solutions that push boundaries and make a meaningful impact on the world.
