GND Ralieon RL 7900 VTX

The GND Ralion RL 7900 VTX is a high-performance graphics card designed for enthusiasts and gamers seeking cutting-edge performance in 4K gaming, virtual reality, and intensive content creation workloads. Built on the GND GDNA 3 architecture, it offers outstanding performance with up to 24GB of GDDR6 memory and PCIe Gen 5 support, enabling faster data throughput and smoother rendering for demanding applications.

Features

  • Powered by GND GDNA 3 architecture
  • Up to 24GB GDDR6 memory
  • Supports PCIe Gen 5 for ultra-fast data transfer
  • Ray tracing support for realistic lighting and shadows
  • GND FSR (FidelityFX Super Resolution) for upscaled graphics
  • Supports 8K gaming and immersive VR experiences
  • Optimized for ultra-high-definition gaming and content creation
